5️⃣ Growth & Mentorship: Your Personalized Roadmap

This plan outlines the six essential stages to go from a music enthusiast to a professional artist or highly skilled instrumentalist:

  • 1. Discover: Take the OKV SOUND quizzes to identify your voice type or best instrument.
  • 2. Train: Commit to daily practice (vocal warm-ups, scale drills, basic chords) for 3-6 months.
  • 3. Practice: Apply theory to real songs. Practice with a metronome and record yourself to track progress.
  • 4. Perform: Start small! Perform for family, join a church choir, or play at local open mic nights. Build confidence.
  • 5. Record: Create high-quality recordings (even simple covers). Build a public portfolio (e.g., YouTube channel, SoundCloud).
  • 6. Monetize: Teach lessons, play paid gigs, compose music for others, or pursue a record deal/producer role.
